Ebonyi State

Ebonyi State is a state in the South-East geopolitical zone of Nigeria, known for its rich agricultural land and mineral resources. The state capital is Abakaliki, and the state is home to a diverse population with a strong cultural heritage. Ebonyi State is often referred to as the "Salt of the Nation" due to its significant salt deposits. The state is also known for its scenic landscapes, including the famous Onuebonyi River and the Cross River. Agriculture is a major economic activity in Ebonyi State, with crops like rice, yams, and cassava being major staples.
